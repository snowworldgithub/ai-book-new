# Course Overview

The **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics** course is designed to provide comprehensive education on the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, with a particular focus on humanoid robotics applications. This course prepares students for careers in advanced robotics research and development.

## Course Structure

The course is organized into seven major modules:

1. **Physical AI Foundations** - Core concepts of intelligence through physical interaction
2. **ROS 2** - Robot Operating System fundamentals and advanced usage
3. **Digital Twin Technologies** - Simulation environments and virtual testing
4. **NVIDIA Isaac Platform** - Advanced AI for robotics applications
5. **Humanoid Robotics Engineering** - Design and control of human-like robots
6. **Vision-Language-Action Systems** - Multimodal AI for physical interaction
7. **Capstone Project** - Integration of all concepts in a comprehensive application

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

- Design and implement AI systems that interact with physical environments
- Develop robotic applications using ROS 2 and modern simulation tools
- Apply advanced AI techniques to humanoid robotics challenges
- Integrate vision, language, and action systems for complex robotic tasks
- Evaluate and optimize robot performance in real-world scenarios
