# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Course

Welcome to the comprehensive course on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This educational platform covers the cutting-edge intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, with a focus on humanoid robotics applications.

## Course Overview

This course provides a complete educational journey through:

- **Physical AI Foundations**: Understanding intelligence that emerges through physical interaction
- **ROS 2**: Robot Operating System for modern robotics development
- **Digital Twin Technologies**: Simulation environments including Gazebo and Unity
- **NVIDIA Isaac Platform**: Advanced AI for robotics applications
- **Humanoid Robotics Engineering**: Design and control of human-like robots
- **Vision-Language-Action Systems**: Multimodal AI for physical interaction
- **Capstone Project**: Integrating all concepts in a comprehensive project

## Learning Approach

Our curriculum emphasizes:
- **Theory-to-Practice**: Each concept includes both theoretical understanding and practical implementation
- **Modular Design**: Flexible learning paths that can be customized to your needs
- **Industry-Aligned**: Content that reflects current best practices and employment requirements
- **Hardware-Software Integration**: Understanding the tight coupling required in physical AI systems

## Getting Started

Begin with the [Course Overview](./course/intro.md) to understand the structure and learning objectives, then proceed through the modules in sequence or focus on specific areas of interest.
